Events & Festivals

Feature the town's annual events and seasonal activities. Replace example info with actual local event details and your photos.

Dauin Fiesta: Scheduled from September 2-10, this fiesta honors San Nicholas de Tolentino and includes various activities such as the search for Miss Dauin 2023, sporting events, and a night market.
Dauin Dive Festival: Taking place from June 25-29, this festival features scuba diving, an underwater photography contest, and a coastal and underwater cleanup. It is a private-led event, marking a shift from previous government-led initiatives.
Kinaiyahan Festival: Celebrated from September 15 to September 30, this festival includes traditional activities such as cockfighting, disco dancing, coconut wine drinking, and an expo. It emphasizes community unity and respect for nature.
Dauin Tourism Week: Scheduled for September 23, this week-long celebration includes a colorful parade, a Boodle Fight competition, and a feast shared by the community. It promotes tourism and sustainable transformation in Dauin.
