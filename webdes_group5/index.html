Dauin

Relax on black-sand beaches and coastal viewpoints perfect for sunsets. Dauin is a coastal municipality in the province of Negros Oriental, Philippines. It is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. The town is a popular destination for diving and snorkeling enthusiasts, with several marine reserves and dive sites along its coast.
